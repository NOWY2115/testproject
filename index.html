Staring from music...

At first I was really into rap music. Don't get me wrong, I'm still a fan of it, but lately I just like experimenting with different genres.

Recently it's been hyperpop and digicore, which struck close to my heart. The synthy vibes, the possibilities for both depressive and positive melodies, the percussion, which is simply just beautiful - those things resonate with me very deeply.

Maybe my music isn't very popular amongst the general audience (at least yet), but I really hope to make it change in the upcoming years ^^. If you want to support me, just try to spread the word of it. I appreciate any support :3

...Through coding...

In case the former thing doesn't work out, I try to make a plan for my future at the moment. I really enjoy coding. I have a more-or-so comprehensible understanding of web development, that means HTML, CSS, JS and a fair share of PHP and SQL. I'm also currently working on improving my knowledge of C#, C++ and Python. If you ever need help with anything simple, feel free to message my mail, I'll see if I can do something for you.

...Even drawing?...

Maybe I'm not an expert in terms of drawing. What's more, I would consider myself a beginner in that area. However, I really enjoy it and it brings a great amount of joy. I don't take any commissions for now

...To English?

I think it's fair to say, that English is a language very close to me. I've been learning it for over a decade now and in 2024 I achieved a C2 Cambrdige certificate. Same as before, if you have any problem translating documents (Polish - English; English-Polish; that's worth noting), also mail me and I'll see what I can do for you.
